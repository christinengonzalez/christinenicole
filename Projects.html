Teenagers

Print. 2017.

Teenagers is a 16 spread remix of Jessie Bernard’s Teen-Age Culture from 1961. Bernard’s book focused on the behavior and culture of teenagers in the early sixties. My goal was to portray teens as a important and contributing individuals in society.

Sweet Like Honey

Website. 2017.

Sweet Like Honey is a visual representation of Rupi Kaur’s untitled poem from Milk and Honey. The website displays a variety of interactive gifs with quotes from the poem. The black and white theme represents the uncertainty of the protagonist in addition to its melancholy story.

How to Buy A BART Ticket

Website. 2017.

How to Buy A BART Ticket is visual guide for any individual on buying a BART ticket. The website is style in a casual, yet playful style that makes it fun to learn about. The color scheme is derived from BART’s map, but slightly toned down.
